## Products & Services

### 1. AI Content API
**What it does:** Generate high-quality written content instantly using AI
**Use cases:**
- Blog posts and articles
- Marketing copy and ads
- Product descriptions
- Social media content
- Email newsletters
- SEO-optimized web content

### 2. Subscription Plans

#### **Basic Plan** - £9.99/month
- 50 AI-generated articles per month
- Up to 1000 words per article
- 3 content styles (blog, marketing, social)
- Standard support
- Perfect for: Small businesses, bloggers, startups

#### **Pro Plan** - £29.99/month  
- 200 AI-generated articles per month
- Up to 2000 words per article
- 6 content styles + custom tones
- Priority support
- API access
- Perfect for: Marketing agencies, content teams, e-commerce

#### **Enterprise Plan** - £99.99/month
- 1000+ AI-generated articles per month
- Unlimited length
- All content styles + brand voice training
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
- Perfect for: Large companies, publishers, SaaS platforms

### 3. Pay-As-You-Go Option
- £0.99 per 1000-word article
- No monthly commitment
- Same AI quality
- Instant generation
